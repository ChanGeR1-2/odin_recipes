Bulgogi

Description

A recipe to make a delicious, yet simple bulgogi dish.

This recipe was written in full by Maangchi.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef sirloin
  • 1/2 cup of crushed Korean pear
  • 4 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon minced ginger
  • 2 tbs soy sauce
  • 2 tbs brown sugar(or 1tbs of brown sugar and 1 tb rice syrup
  • 1 tbs toasted sesame oil
  • several thin slices of carrot

Steps

  1. Mix all the marinade ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Add the sliced beef and mix well.
  3. It is best to let the meat marinate for at least 30 minutes in the fridge, but you can also cook it immediately.
  4. Grill over medium-high heat
  5. Serve with Korean side dishes, like kimchi, lettuce, ssamjang, and garlic.
