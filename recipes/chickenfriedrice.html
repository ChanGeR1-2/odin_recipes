Chicken Fried Rice

Description

A quick and easy dish that anyone can make.

The detailed instructions for this dish can be found on BBC Good Food.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs sunflower oil
  • 3 eggs, beaten with some seasoning
  • 320g pack mixed stir-fry vegetable
  • 1 tbs mild curry powder
  • 140g frozen sweetcorn
  • 600g cooked rice
  • 1 roasted chicken breast, finely shredded
  • 2 tbs soy sauce
  • 2 tbs sweet chilli sauce

Steps

  1. Heat a splash of oil in a large frying pan and tip in the beaten eggs.
  2. Swirl the pan to coat in a thin layer of egg and cook for a few mins until set.
  3. Tip onto a chopping board, roll up, slice thinly and set aside.
  4. Heat a little more oil, add the stir-fry veg, curry powder and sweetcorn with a splash of water. Cook for 1-2 mins until the veg starts to wilt, then tip into a bowl.
  5. Add the last of the oil to the pan, tip in the rice and chicken, mix well, then add the soy sauce, sweet chilli, ketchup, a splash of water and some black pepper.
  6. Finally, add the eggs and the veg, toss together and heat through until hot. Tip into bowls and serve immediately.
